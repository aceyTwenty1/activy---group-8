# ⚡ ACTÍVY — Group 8

Actívy is an AI-powered fitness tracking engine that records video workouts, tracks body movements in real time, counts repetitions, and grades overall form.

---

## 🔍 Core Features

* **Body Tracking:** Scans video data frame-by-frame to map 33 coordinates across the body, focusing on key joints like shoulders, hips, knees, and ankles.
* **Biomechanical Mathematics:** Calculates the precise angular degrees and spatial alignment of these coordinates as they move through 3D space.
* **Instant Feedback Engine:** Compares movement data against a database of specific execution rules for **10 different exercises** (Squats, Push-Ups, Lunges, Bicep Curls, etc.).
* **Form Diagnostics:** Automatically detects faults—such as shallow depth, a sagging core, or bad posture—and outputs a clean report showing total reps, form scores, and specific coaching cues.
